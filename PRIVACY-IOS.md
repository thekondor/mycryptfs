# Privacy Policy for MyCryptFS Mobile

**Last Updated**: April 2026

## Overview

MyCryptFS Mobile (aka just `MyCryptFS` depending on the context) is an iOS application for accessing encrypted volumes.
This policy describes what information the app handles and how.

## Data Collected

MyCryptFS does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

The app stores the following **locally on your device only**:

- **Encrypted folder paths** — file system paths you configure, stored in macOS App Group UserDefaults on your device.
- **Passwords** — stored exclusively in the macOS Keychain on your device. Passwords are never written to disk in plaintext, never stored in UserDefaults, and never transmitted over any network.

## Data Not Collected

- No analytics or usage tracking
- No crash reporting sent to any server
- No identifiers, diagnostics, or telemetry
- No network connections of any kind

## Third Parties

MyCryptFS does not share any data with third parties. No third-party services, SDKs, or frameworks are used for data collection, analytics, advertising, or any form of data processing. The app makes no network requests.

## Data Retention

Passwords and folder paths remain on your device until you remove them within the app or uninstall the app. Uninstalling the app does not automatically remove Keychain entries — you can delete them manually via Keychain Access.
